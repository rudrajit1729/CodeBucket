Brazil’s beauty is no secret to anyone. At least once in a lifetime, you must experience this beauty with your own eyes. If you haven’t seen this place yet, then you better buckle up your seatbelts because you won’t be able to resist this.
Brazil, colloquially known as Brasil, is famous for its football tradition and its annual Carnival in Rio de Janeiro. It is a country of great diversity, from the bustling urban mosaic of São Paulo to the infinite cultural energy of Pernambuco and Bahia, the wilderness of the Amazon rainforest and world-class landmarks such as the Iguaçu Falls, there is plenty to see and to do in Brazil.
